What is Nutrigenetics?

While Nutrigenomics studies how food affects your gene expression, Nutrigenetics focuses on how your inherited genetic variations dictate your individual response to the food you eat.

By understanding your specific genetic makeup, we can pinpoint exactly which foods you thrive on, which ones you should avoid, and how your body metabolizes caffeine, lactose, and gluten.
