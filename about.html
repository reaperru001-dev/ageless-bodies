About Ageless Bodies

🌱 Our Story

From a Vision to 15,000+ Lives Changed

Ageless Bodies was founded in 2014 by Dr. Prabhat Rau, a PhD in Nutritional Genomics from IIT Bombay, after she experienced firsthand how generic diet plans fail. Her DNA analysis revealed she was lactose intolerant and had a MTHFR gene variant — knowledge that transformed her own health journey.

What started as a small clinic in Mumbai has grown into India's leading personalized nutrition platform, with 50+ certified nutritionists serving clients across 20+ cities and 8 countries.

Our mission is simple: No more one-size-fits-all diets. Every person deserves a nutrition plan as unique as their DNA.
