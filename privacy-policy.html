Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2026

1. Introduction

At Ageless Bodies, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and genetic information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and address when you create an account or book a consultation.
  • Health Information: Medical history, current diet, lifestyle habits, and blood work reports provided during consultations.
  • Genetic Information: Data extracted from your saliva sample if you opt for our DNA-Based nutrition plan.

3. How We Use Your Genetic Data

Your genetic data is used strictly for the purpose of creating your personalized nutrition and lifestyle plan. It is anonymized at our partner laboratories. We do not sell, rent, or lease your genetic data to any third party, including pharmaceutical companies or insurance providers.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption (AES-256) and strict access controls to protect your data. All health and genetic records are stored on HIPAA-compliant servers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to your data, request corrections, or ask for the complete deletion of your account and genetic information from our servers at any time.
